# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically constructive community. Contributions should engage with ideas and evidence without attacking people. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and disclosure of private information are not acceptable.

## Scientific Discussion

Disagreement about methods, interpretation, or terminology is welcome when it is specific, evidence-based, and proportionate. Contributors must clearly distinguish educational simulations, cached artifacts, and scientific predictions.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
